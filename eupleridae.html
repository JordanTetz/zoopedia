Kingdom: Animalia

Genus: ---

Classification

Eupleridae belongs the group of carnivorans known as "Feliformia", which encompasses all catlike carnivorans, characterized by a shorter snout than caniforms. Other families of Feliformia include Felidae, Prionodontidae, and Nandiniidae. Furthermore, Eupleridae belongs to Viverroidea, which encompasses families closely related genetically, those being Hyaenidae, Viverridae, Herpestidae, and itself.

Description

Eupleridae, also known as Malagasy Mongooses, are mongooses native to Madagascar. There are few physical characteristics that are common among them. They have slender bodies, short legs, long tais and blunt faces. Many members of this family are arboreal and are therefore strong climbers.
